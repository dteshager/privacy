Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 25, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

• Account Information: username, email, and optional profile details.
• Content You Provide: posts, messages, images, and reports you submit.
• Usage and Device Data: app interactions, approximate location you enter on posts, device identifiers, and diagnostic logs to improve reliability.
• Communications: messages you send through the app and with support.

How We Use Information

• Provide and improve core features like posting, search, chat, and notifications.
• Safety and moderation, including handling reports and preventing abuse/spam.
• Communicate with you about your account, changes, and feature updates.
• Analyze usage trends to improve performance and user experience.

Sharing

• With other users for content you choose to post or messages you send.
• With service providers (e.g., hosting, storage, analytics) under contractual safeguards.
• For legal, safety, and security purposes when required by law or to protect users.

Your Choices

• You may access, update, or delete your posts. You can delete your account at any time from settings.
• Control notifications via your device settings.
• Do not share sensitive personal information in posts or messages.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. Deleted accounts remove profile and posts; certain logs may be retained for fraud and safety.
